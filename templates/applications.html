Real-World Applications

Object recognition technology is transforming various industries. Here are some key applications:

  • Autonomous Vehicles:
    Enables self-driving cars to perceive and react to their environment.
  • Surveillance:
    Enhances security and monitoring through automated object detection.
  • Retail:
    Improves inventory management, automates checkout processes.
  • Healthcare:
    Aids in medical imaging analysis and diagnostics.
  • Manufacturing:
    Automates quality control and inspection processes.
